Matlab Inrimage allows to read and write images in the INRIMAGE-4 file format (although only a subset of the possible ones). Uncompress and untar the archive and follow the instructions in the readme.txt file.

Matlab EPS allows to save matlab 2D arrays and 3D arrays (with third dimension = 3) directly as Encapsulated Postrcipt images, without going through the figure functions. Uncompress and untar the archive and follow the instructions in the readme.txt file.

Some notes on the Calculus of Variations in Image Analysis. They were originally written in French for a course at FittingBox, and translated into English (with the appreciated help of Aasa Feragen). They are still in draft mode, but could be useful (they have been for some students at DIKU and at FittinBox).
